esda.shape.length_width_diff¶

esda.shape.length_width_diff(collection)[source]¶

The Eig & Seitzinger (1981) shape measure, defined as:

L - W

Where L is the maximal east-west extent and W is the maximal north-south extent.

Defined as measure LW_5 in [Alt98]
